Introduction

This is a group project, other two members are Zirou Qiu and Diejie Gao. The project report is here.

In this project, we focus on the cultural resource allocation of New York City. We use the dataset provided by FourSquare which consists of the venue check-in history of users. After exploring many related works on pattern extraction, spatial data clustering, and optimization, we choose algorithms suggested by Zhou et al.

I construct the demand-supply model using Gaussian function. The demand level of one venue is defined by the probability of users going to that venue.
