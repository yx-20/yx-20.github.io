Bank Marketing Analysis

Introduction

The bank has high demand on knowing whether a client is a potential customer or not at a lower cost. The project aims to help bank predict the potential customers list. In advance, label the potential customers' level to express difficulty. The higher the level, the more time need to spend to successfully marketing.

Dataset

The dataset is from a Portuguese Banking Institution with 41188 examples. Each example has 20 attributes include the personal information, marketing information and financial information.
