Alexa Command Security

Background

Alexa help customers to perform everyday tasks or engage with your content naturally with voice. But it still has some security risks:

  • Alexa not only answer the question you ask but also ask you questions for more information.
  • What alexa says may control other device in your home.
  • You can not identify the skill you are using.

This is a group project, other two members are Diejie Gao and Huixing Deng. The project report is here.

Work

1. Build an “Alexa conversations” dataset

2. Explore the dataset, find out risks under conversation:

  • Skills may disobey Alexa policy
  • Alexa may cause privacy issue by asking questions
  • Alexa may have unknown problem to leak users’ information
