Physically Based Animation

Introduction

This project implements particle systems, flocking, soft body simulation and rigid body simulation. Translate mathematical and physical conceptes into software.

The game backgrounds are very simple and aim to display different animations. The player can control the white object and interact with environments.

The following two figures show Flow of Dynamical Simulation and Flow of PBA Code. These two figures are from course website.
